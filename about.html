About

As students in CP202, we were frustrated by the lack of learning material and useful content provided through Laurier's mylearningspace. We set out to create an innovative and immersive website where students of CP202 could collaborate and work on problems together. After some critical surveying of CP202 students, we decided to not only provide class content, but also learning activities to solidify the information being taken in throughout each chapter. In addition to learning activities, we discovered students also wanted to test their knowledge in order to understand where they needed improvement. As a result we added practice test that are updated on an onoing basis as new class notes are contributed. Lastly, in order to keep every resource on one site, we provided students with references to other important website design regulators and standards. Join us in our attempt to bring students together to accomplish learning goals. We are students helping students.
